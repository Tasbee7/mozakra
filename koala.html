KOALA













  • Scientific name: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Life cycle : 13-18 years (in the wild)
  • Mass: 4 - 15 kg (adult animal)
  • Height: 60 - 85 cm (adult animal)

Koala or gray koala is a type of mammal that follows the gender of the follicular bear, the species of follicular bears. It is an animal with open bursa from the back, it is found in Australia, and it usually lives on trees.
